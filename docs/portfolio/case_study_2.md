# Case Study #2: B2B Sales Lead Intelligence at Scale

## Problem

A B2B sales team at a mid-market SaaS company spent 45 minutes per prospect researching company backgrounds, identifying decision makers, and scoring fit. With a pipeline of 50 new leads per week, this consumed 37.5 hours of SDR time weekly — time that could be spent on actual outreach.

## Solution

Using the Agentic Research Factory with the **B2B Sales Lead Intel** vertical:

1. **Input**: Company URL, target buyer role ("VP Engineering"), product value prop ("API monitoring platform")
2. **Process**: The system automatically:
   - Scraped the company homepage and /about page via Firecrawl
   - Searched for funding news, leadership team, and tech stack via Tavily
   - Built a structured prospect dossier with the LeadIntel agent
   - Scored company fit on a 1–10 scale with cited reasoning
3. **Output**: A structured dossier with company overview, key decision makers (with LinkedIn links), recent news, technology stack, fit score, and recommended outreach angle.

## Results

| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Time per prospect | 45 min | ~3 min |
| SDR hours/week on research | 37.5 hrs | 2.5 hrs |
| Decision makers identified | 1–2 avg | 3–5 avg |
| Fit score accuracy | Gut feel | Evidence-based (cited) |
| Cost per dossier | ~$25 (SDR time) | ~$0.20 (API costs) |

## Key Takeaway

The platform eliminated 93% of manual prospect research time while improving the depth and consistency of lead intelligence. SDRs now spend their time on personalised outreach instead of copy-pasting from LinkedIn.
