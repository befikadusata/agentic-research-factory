# Case Study #1: Deep Research Report for a Series A SaaS Founder

## Problem

A Series A SaaS founder needed to evaluate the competitive landscape for AI-powered legal tech targeting SMB law firms. Traditional research approaches took 2–3 weeks using junior analysts and cost $3,000–$5,000 per report. The founder needed actionable intelligence within 24 hours to inform a board meeting.

## Solution

Using the Agentic Research Factory with the **Founder Strategy Brief** vertical:

1. **Input**: Market segment ("AI-powered legal tech for SMBs"), stage ("Seed"), key question ("Is now the right time to enter the SMB legal market?")
2. **Process**: The system automatically:
   - Searched 8 sources via Tavily (funding databases, market reports, competitor sites)
   - Scraped 3 key pages via Firecrawl (competitor pricing pages, industry reports)
   - Synthesized findings through a 4-agent pipeline (Researcher → Analyst → Writer → Editor)
   - Paused for human review at the HITL checkpoint
   - Generated a structured strategy brief with required sections
3. **Output**: A 2,000-word strategy brief with market sizing, competitive mapping (7 players), timing analysis, and a direct answer to the key strategic question.

## Results

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Time to completion | ~8 minutes |
| Citations in output | 12 external sources |
| Accuracy score (LLM-as-Judge) | 8.2/10 |
| Relevance score | 9.1/10 |
| Cost per report | ~$0.35 (API costs) |

## Key Takeaway

The platform reduced a 2-week, $4,000 research cycle to an 8-minute, $0.35 automated workflow — with HITL quality control ensuring the founder could trust the output for board-level decisions.
